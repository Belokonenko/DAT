Plant protection

Means of plant protection are divided into four main types: mechanical, chemical, biological and agrotechnical. However, the most rational thing is their simultaneous, correct combination. The most common method is chemical. There are thousands of different types of chemical protection products. But they should not be confused, let alone combined with chemical fertilizers. The methods of their influence on plants are completely opposite.

It is necessary to know and distinguish chemical means of plant protection. After all, these drugs have a strictly defined object of influence (the causative agent of the disease, insect pests, a species or family of weeds).

Poisonous chemicals do not tolerate negligence. This is the type of drug that is not enough to simply dilute in water and spray plants with it. If pesticides are used incorrectly, they can harm you, the beneficial insects, and the crop.
